關於作者  

作者是第3代基督徒，他在移居加拿大之前在青少年時期接受了基督。他得了土木工程的教育，並註冊為專業工程師（目前為退休成員）。畢業後，他在多倫多的一家建築諮詢公司工作了幾年，然後被阿爾伯塔省勞工部安全與服務部-建築標準處聘為建築標準官員。後來，他加入了聯邦政府的消防專員辦公室 (Office of the Fire Commissioner) ，並獲得了高級工程師的職稱。返回安大略省後，他的工作涉及為加拿大財政部起草安全和建築政策和法規，管理聯邦政府擁有或租賃的建築物。他代表加拿大財政部檢查加拿大海外大使館和世界博覽會裡的加拿大館等其他建築是否符合加拿大標準。他是保險商實驗室和加拿大標準協會等標準編寫組織的委員會成員。2011年3月，他被加拿大總督大衛約翰斯頓授予消防服務模範服務獎章。他在退休前擔任聯邦消防局副局長和代理總工程師。

About the author

The author is a 3rd generation Christian, who accepted Christ in his teenage years before emigrating to Canada. He received his education in civil engineering and is registered as a professional engineer (currently as a retired member). After graduation, he worked for a consulting firm for several years in Toronto before joining the Alberta Labour General Safety & Services Division - Building Standards Branchas a Building Standard Officer. Later, he joined the Office of the Fire Commissioner with the federal government and held the title of Senior Engineer. After returning to Ontario, his work involved the drafting of safety and construction policies and regulations, governing buildings and structures owned or leased by the federal government, for the Treasury Board of Canada. On behalf of the Treasury Board of Canada, He inspected overseas Canadian embassies and other building structures such as Canadian Pavilions at the World Expositions for compliance of Canadian standards. He was a committee member of standard writing organizations such as Underwriters’ Laboratories and Canadian Standard Association. He was awarded the Fire Services Exemplary Service Medal from the Governor General of Canada David Johnston in March 2011. He held the positions of Deputy Federal Fire Commissioner and Acting Chief Engineer before his retirement.
